Team Aetheris Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 17, 2025

Your privacy is paramount to us. This policy outlines how Team Aetheris ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

  1. Information We Collect
    We only collect the following types of information:
    • Information You Provide: Name, email address, username, and other details submitted during account registration.
    • Automatically Collected Information: IP address, browser type, access timestamps, and device information obtained via cookies and similar technologies.
    • Third-Party Data: Public profile information obtained through social media logins (where applicable).
  2. How We Use Your Information
    Your information may be used to:
    • Deliver and improve our services (e.g., account management, technical support);
    • Send critical notifications (e.g., policy updates, security alerts);
    • Analyze website usage to enhance user experience;
    • Fulfill legal compliance obligations.
  3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
    We do not sell your data, but may share it under these circumstances:
    • Service Providers: With partners performing hosting, analytics, or payment processing functions (all bound by confidentiality agreements);
    • Legal Compliance: To respond to court orders or governmental requests;
    • Security Protection: To prevent fraud, address security threats, or protect our legitimate rights.
  4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
    We use cookies to:
    • Maintain user login sessions;
    • Analyze traffic and optimize website performance;
    • Deliver personalized content (e.g., advertising preferences).
    You may disable cookies via browser settings, though some features may be impaired.
  5. Data Security
    We implement reasonable safeguards (including encryption and access controls), but cannot guarantee absolute security.
  6. Your Rights
    Under applicable laws, you may have the right to:
    • Access, correct, or delete personal information;
    • Restrict or object to data processing;
    • Request data portability;
    • Withdraw processing consent (contact us to exercise).
  7. Children's Privacy
    Our services are not directed at children under 13. We will promptly delete any inadvertently collected data.
  8. International Data Transfers
    For cross-border data transfers, we ensure compliance with regulations like GDPR through mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
  9. Policy Updates
    We will notify users of revisions via website notices or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
  10. Contact Us
    For inquiries or rights requests:
    Email: teamaetheris@outlook.com
